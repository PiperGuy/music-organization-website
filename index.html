Welcome to Ananda Dhwani!

The Ananda Dhwani Music Trust primarily focusses on the treasures of Classical Music. It will bring forth the joys of classical music through performances, talent searches and furthering education and training.

Our Goals
  • Organize world class concerts
  • Organize talent hunts
  • Training camps
  • Continued training
  • Facilitate musicians to pursue education
  • Publish educational and training materials

About Us

Vision: Music is breath

Ananda Dhwani literally means Happy Melody! Happiness and Peace through music is what Ananda Dhwani symbolizes. Happiness that a Guru gets in seeing his student perform. The happiness of breaking into a song. Happiness of composing soul stirring music. Happiness of facilitating high potential musicians and the list goes on. It is not unknown to the world, that some of the greatest musicians found solace in music in their darkest hours. Music is therapy for the crying soul, music is a lover for the lonely soul, music is celebration for the happy soul. “I listen to music when I am happy and I listen to music when I am sad”.

You could be a professional or an amateur, a student, a teacher or an audience, but if its music which makes you smile, you are part of the world of Ananda Dhawni
